Value Proposition:

We organize corporate team-building events to transform urban green spaces

giving companies real ESG impact, great employer branding, and employees an outdoor experience they'll actually remember.

How it works

Source the Space

We partner with districts, schools & landowners to identify grey zones and secure permits for greening.

→

The Action Day

Corporate teams spend 4 hours assembling parklets, planting greenery, painting heat-reflective surfaces, guided by our urbanist expert and our PR team.

→

The Green Legacy

A finished micro-park is handed to the community. The company gets a plaque, ESG data & employer branding content.
